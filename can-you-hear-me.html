Can You Hear Me? (2026)

Editorial & Bookbinding

This 28-page brochure documents profoundly deaf student Lilly Cracknell's navigation of systemic barriers, revealing the invisible labor, technology failures, and institutional resistance hearing people rarely see. Through interview testimony and disability research, it exposes the gap between accessibility claims and lived reality through professional, manual-style design with typographic choices that reject sympathy narratives and center deaf capability and expertise.
